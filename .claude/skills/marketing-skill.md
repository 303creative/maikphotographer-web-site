# Marketing Skill — Content & Strategy

## Objetivo
Guiar agentes para crear contenido de marketing auténtico y conversión-enfocado.

---

## 1. TONE OF VOICE

### Maikel Marshall Brand Voice
- **Cinematic**: Evoca movimiento, luz, composición
- **Authentic**: Directo, sin marketing-speak genérico
- **Aspirational**: Inspira sin ser pretencioso
- **Journalistic**: Cuenta historias reales de clientes

### Ejemplos ✅
```
"Cinematic portraits and editorial photography"
"I don't just take photos. I direct."
"Cuban-born creative director based in Miami"
```

### Ejemplos ❌
```
"Best photographer in Miami" (genérico)
"Professional photo services" (aburrido)
"Award-winning photographer" (sin contexto)
```

## 2. COPYWRITING TEMPLATES

### Hero Section
**Pattern**: `[Verb] [Emotion]. [Outcome]`

```
"Shoot real. Feel it."
"Create stories that sell"
"Direct your vision"
```

### Service Descriptions
**Pattern**: `[Who] [Benefit]. [Detail about process]`

```
Portrait: "Individual or couples portrait session in Miami. 
One or two locations. Edited gallery delivered within 48 hours."

Editorial: "Full editorial production for models, creators and brands. 
Concept, art direction, location scouting, full team coordination."

Brand: "Monthly content package for brands and businesses. 
Social media visuals, product photography, Instagram-ready delivery."
```

### About Section
**Pattern**: `[Origin]. [Expertise]. [Philosophy]`

```
"Cuban-born cinematic photographer based in Miami, FL.
Founder of the303 Creative. 5+ years blending visual 
storytelling with strategic marketing.
Specialized in portrait, editorial, lifestyle with distinctive 
filmic aesthetic."
```

## 6. MARKETING SECTIONS STRATEGY

### Hero
- **Goal**: Capture attention in 3 seconds
- **Method**: Cinematic visuals + one-liner tagline
- **CTA**: Primary (Book) + Secondary (Portfolio)

### About
- **Goal**: Build trust & authority
- **Method**: Story (origin) + expertise + philosophy
- **Elements**: Photo + bio + stats + CTA

### Portfolio
- **Goal**: Show quality & range
- **Method**: Curated images + categories + hover stories
- **Elements**: 9 images + filterable + full IG link

### Marketing Projects
- **Goal**: Demonstrate client success
- **Method**: Before/after or project showcase
- **Elements**: 6 major clients + categories + details

### Services
- **Goal**: Clear pricing + packages
- **Method**: Three tiers (entry, popular, premium)
- **Elements**: Features list + price + CTA per service

### Contact
- **Goal**: Multiple ways to engage
- **Method**: Direct contact + form + booking
- **Elements**: WhatsApp + Email + Instagram + Cal.com

## 8. TONE EXAMPLES FOR AGENTS

### When Writing Copy
```
NO:  "High-quality professional photography services with 
      award-winning photographer offering competitive rates 
      and fast turnaround"

YES: "Cinematic portraits and editorial photography for 
      models, creators and brands. Cuban-born creative 
      director based in Miami."
```

### When Describing Services
```
NO:  "Photography packages available starting at $150 
      with various options and add-ons"

YES: "Portrait Session — Individual or couples portrait 
      session in Miami. 1–2 hours, one or two locations. 
      Edited gallery delivered within 48 hours."
```

### When Building Case Studies
```
NO:  "Great project, client was very happy with results, 
      photos turned out really well"

YES: "Velora Miami — Complete brand identity and 
      marketing photography for luxury real estate. 
      Result: 40% increase in qualified leads."
```

## 12. AGENT TASKS

When working on marketing improvements:

1. **Analyze** current messaging
2. **Identify** what's generic vs authentic
3. **Rewrite** using templates above
4. **Test** copy against audience
5. **Measure** conversion impact
6. **Iterate** based on results

**Remember**: Every word should serve conversion or brand building.
