Frequently Asked

What is included in the session?
All sessions include the photographer's time on location, full editing, an online gallery with print-ready files, and a 48-hour delivery turnaround.
Do you travel outside Miami?
Yes! I'm available for travel. Travel sessions require a higher rate and minimum 3-day booking. Let's discuss your location in the inquiry form.
Can I book a custom package?
Absolutely. If none of the standard packages fit your needs, I'm happy to create a custom quote for multi-day shoots, larger teams, or specific requirements.
What's your payment & cancellation policy?
A 50% deposit secures your date, balance due on the day of the shoot. Cancellations within 14 days forfeit the deposit. Rescheduling is always possible.
How quickly can I get my photos?
Standard turnaround is 48 hours for edited galleries. Rush delivery (24 hours) is available for +$50. Your files include JPG, RAW, and print-ready formats.
