   - Trim a different section than TikTok (audience may overlap)
   - Music: Instagram-optimized trending audio

   - Link in bio

4. **Instagram Carousel** (5-7 slides)
   - Screenshot key frames
   - Text overlay with tips
   - Before/after if applicable

5. **LinkedIn Article**
   - 800-1000 word written version
   - Professional tone
   - Include images from video
   - CTA: Book session or consult

   - 1200-1500 words
   - Embed YouTube video
   - SEO keywords: "how to pose for portraits", "cinematic portrait posing"
   - Link to booking page

7. **Email Newsletter** (if exists)
